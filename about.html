About ZenPlay

Your Ultimate Gaming Destination

Fridah Kirima

Owner of ZenPlay Gaming Lounge

ZenPlay Gaming Lounge was born from Fridah Kirima's passion for gaming and a vision to create the ultimate gaming community hub. Since our inception, we have set out to build a space where gamers of all levels can come together, play, compete, and connect.

We are proud to share our passion across two premier locations: our lounge at Sayen Makutano and Gitimbine at Selenite Mall. Both locations feature state-of-the-art equipment, premium accessories, and immersive VR experiences.

Our mission is simple: to provide the best gaming experience possible — whether you're visiting one of our lounges for a casual session, competing in a tournament, or shopping for the latest gear.
